Low Poly Character Design Blender

Overview

I created a low-polygon character for a computer game. I will include modelling the character and then rigging the character to produce different animations for when they are idle, running, and etc.

End Users

The people who buy my game a re people who like pirates, adventure, and boats. The age range of my game is about from 11 to 20, and the country interest is probably places in europe. and the people who would by my character is someone who would want to make a low poly pirate game.

Requirements and specifications

  • Head: Pirate Hat

  • Body: square body

  • legs: wooden leg and normal leg

  • sword: long skinny saber

  • eyes: 2 blocks on top of eachother

  • mouth: a cylinder on the face
